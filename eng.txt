Present simple : routine (evry day) , in general, usually
  : ( he , she , it )  verb + S => Sam drinks coffee => We like spring
? : Do + subject (“I, you, we, they”) + verb => Do I drink coffee every morning
? : Does + subject (“he, she, it”) + verb (no S) => why does Paul often work late?
- : Do not (don’t) or does not + verb (no s) =>I do not (don’t) work .. => My mom does not (doesn’t) like ...)

Present Progressive (continuous) :  dans se moment
  : Subject + be (i : am - he, she, it : is - they , you , we : are ) + verb(ing) => bark is sleeping on my bed.
? : be + the subject =>is he playing ?
- : Be + not + verb(ing) => you aren’t


 present perfect : on peux pas poser la question when , on parle d'une experience passer generale sans savoir quand exactement, il commence dans le passe est ne finie pas 
 : Subject + auxiliary verb (have(i,you,we,they)(i've) or has(he,she,it) (he's) + verb (past participle : regular verb+ed 3d verb) => I have bought a new car
 ? : The auxiliary verb( have or has) + subject => have you ever been to Brazil ?
 - : have not or haven't , has not or hasn't => I haven't heard from him in a while.
 
  present perfect continuous : debit dans le passer jusqu'a le moment de parler 
 : Subject + auxiliary verb (have(i,you,we,they)(i've) or has(he,she,it) (he's) + been + verb + ing  => i have been waiting for you
? : have/has + i/he + been + verb + ing ? => have you been waiting for me all the time?
-: haven't/hasn't +  been + verb + ing =>i haven't been waiting for her anymore 

Simple past : action passe fini
 : Subject + verb(d or ed) + object => I worked very hard last year (sauf irregular verb)
 ? : Did (helping verb) + subject + verb (present form)  : Did you tell ..?
 - : Did not , didn’t + (verb infinitif) (without ed) => I didn't steal your bubble gum 


 Past Progressive (continuous): actions progressistes en cours qui ne sont pas encore terminées à un moment donné dans le passé. (while)
 : Subject + be (past: was/were) + verb(ing) , Be : past (was : I, he , she, it) (were : you, we, they) => I was working all night.
 ? : Be + subject + verb(ing) => what were you doing at that bar
 - : Was not, wasn’t / Were not, weren’t => shut up. I wasn't talking to you
 
  Past perfect : Il permet d'exprimer une action accomplie antérieure à un moment donné situé dans le passé : 
 : subject + the auxiliary(had) + past participle of the verb  3rd (verb) => when he arrived , the phone had already been repared
 ? : Be + subject + verb(ing) => what were you doing at that bar
 - : Was not, wasn’t / Were not, weren’t => shut up. I wasn't talking to you
 
simple future :
 : Subject + auxulliary verb (will)+ verb => I will(i'll)tell you what happened but only if you don't laught
 ? : Will (auxulliary verb) + subject+ verb => Will you help me or what ?
 - :  will not (won’t) => I won't tell anyone Don't worry
